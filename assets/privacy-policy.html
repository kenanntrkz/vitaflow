VitaFlow — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

1. Information We Collect

VitaFlow collects the following information to provide our resume-building service:

  • Account Information: Email address, name, and encrypted password when you create an account.
  • Resume Data: Personal details, work experience, education, skills, and other information you provide while building your resume.
  • Usage Data: App usage patterns, feature interactions, and AI generation counts for rate limiting purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To store and retrieve your resume data
  • To generate AI-powered content suggestions using third-party AI services
  • To perform ATS (Applicant Tracking System) compatibility checks
  • To manage your subscription and premium features

3. AI Processing

When you use AI features, portions of your resume data are sent to Anthropic's Claude AI service for content generation and optimization. This data is processed in real-time and is not stored by the AI provider beyond the request lifecycle.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers with encryption at rest and in transit. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt. Authentication tokens are stored securely on your device.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Anthropic (Claude AI): For AI-powered content generation
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management
  • Google Play: For app distribution and billing

6. Your Rights

  • Access your data at any time through the app
  • Export your resume as PDF
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of AI features

7. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, all personal data is permanently removed within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

VitaFlow is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes

We may update this policy and will notify you through the app. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at: privacy@vitaflow.app
